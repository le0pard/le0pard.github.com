Shake&Mute - is an application that allows you to disable the phone ring during an incoming call, simply shake or slap the phone. You can shake the bag, which contains the phone or slam on the pocket. Sensor sensitivity adjustable by calibration.

get for free

Features

  • Mute sound of call by accelerometer
  • Auto and manual calibration of the sensitivity
  • Leave a vibration when a call
  • Clean and simple UI

Requirements

  • Android 2.0.1 or higher
