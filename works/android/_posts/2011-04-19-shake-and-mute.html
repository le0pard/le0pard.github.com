Shake&Mute - is an application that allows you to disable the phone ring during an incoming call. You should only shake or slap the phone. If the phone in your bag you can shake it or your can slam the pocket for mute. Sensor sensitivity is adjustable by calibration.

get for free

Features

  • Mute sound of call by accelerometer
  • Auto and manual calibration of the sensitivity
  • Leave a vibration when a call
  • Clean and simple UI

Requirements

  • Android 2.0.1 or higher
